About Anime

Anime is a Japanese style of animation that's known for its colorful graphics, energetic characters, and action-packed plots.
Shimokawa Oten is considered one of the fathers of anime, and was among the first producers.
From romance and comedy to action and adventure, anime covers a wide spectrum of genres.
Attack on Titan, Naruto, and One Piece are just a few of the manga (Japanese comics) adaptations.
